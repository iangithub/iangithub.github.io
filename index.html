LINE Chatbot · 2020

敗口罩"Buy Mask" Bot

疫情期間開發的 LINE Chatbot，民眾只要傳送所在位置，即可即時查到鄰近實名制口罩販售藥局的名稱、地址與電話。為解決政府公開資料僅有地址的問題，另行開發地址轉座標程式以計算距離並推薦最近藥局；服務建構於 Microsoft Azure 之上，能在尖峰流量下快速恢復。

A LINE chatbot built during the pandemic: users share their location and instantly get nearby pharmacies selling rationed masks (name, address, phone). Since the open data only had addresses, I built an address-to-coordinates pipeline to compute distance and recommend the closest stores — all running on Microsoft Azure for resilience under peak load.

LINE Chatbot · 2022

找找快篩試劑"Find Rapid Test" Bot

以 Microsoft Azure AI 為核心、一天內快速上線的 LINE Chatbot，協助民眾查詢附近藥局的快篩試劑庫存資訊。延續「敗口罩」的雲端與資料能力，由微軟新聞中心專文報導，累積使用人數持續攀升，與「敗口罩」合計服務超過百萬名使用者。

A LINE chatbot powered by Microsoft Azure AI, shipped within a single day, helping people check rapid-test-kit availability at nearby pharmacies. Featured by the Microsoft News Center, it extended the cloud-and-data approach of "Buy Mask" — together serving over a million users.
